# Code of conduct

We want Perseval's community to be precise, kind, and safe for people with
different backgrounds and levels of experience.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and sustained
personal attacks are not acceptable. Technical disagreement is welcome when it
focuses on evidence and the work rather than the person.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, comments, or participation that
violate these expectations. Report conduct concerns privately through the
process in `SECURITY.md`; do not litigate sensitive reports in public issues.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when representing the project in
other public spaces.
